Our Rooms & Spaces

Discover the perfect blend of luxury and culture.

Luxurious Bedroom

Experience the best night of your life in our luxurious bedroom, designed to provide ultimate comfort and relaxation. Located in the heart of the Maasai Mara, this room offers a serene ambiance, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure. The room features a king-size bed with premium bedding, a private balcony overlooking the savannah, and an en-suite bathroom with a rain shower. Modern amenities include high-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a smart TV. The decor combines contemporary luxury with traditional Maasai elements, creating a unique and authentic experience.

Cozy Bedroom

Our cozy bedroom is designed to transport you into a world of tranquility. With a perfect blend of modern amenities and traditional Maasai decor, this room ensures a restful night's sleep under the African stars. This intimate space features a queen-size bed with plush bedding, a reading nook with comfortable seating, and a private bathroom with a bathtub. The room includes climate control, a mini-fridge, and a coffee maker. The traditional Maasai artwork and textiles add warmth and cultural authenticity to your stay.

Elegant Living Room

Relax in our elegant living room, where comfort meets style. This spacious area is perfect for socializing with fellow travelers or simply enjoying a quiet moment with a book. The Maasai-inspired decor adds a touch of cultural richness to your stay. Serene Veranda

Unwind on our cozy veranda, where you can enjoy breathtaking views of the Maasai Mara. Whether it's sipping your morning coffee or watching the sunset, this space is perfect for relaxing and soaking in the beauty of nature. The veranda offers comfortable outdoor seating, including hammocks and lounge chairs. It features a covered area for shade during the day and is equipped with ambient lighting for evening relaxation.
